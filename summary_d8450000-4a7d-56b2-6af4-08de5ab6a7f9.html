Package Overview

"Embark on a spiritually enriching journey carefully designed to deliver a Hajj experience that combines serenity, organization, and profound spiritual meaning. This program prioritizes pilgrim comfort, allowing ample time for worship, reflection, and the performance of sacred rituals with peace of mind. The journey begins in Madinah with accommodation at Assaafa Hotel – 4-star standard, enjoying a location within close proximity to Al Masjid An-Nabawi, offering comfortable rooms and a calm atmosphere that supports spiritual focus, in addition to an enriching visit to selected historical landmarks. After completing the Madinah program, pilgrims proceed to Makkah via modern buses or the HHR High-Speed Train, depending on availability. The program continues in Makkah with accommodation at Rafahiat Al Mashaer Hotel – 1-star standard, located in the Al Aziziyah district, offering practical accommodation with essential services in a well-organized environment that supports a smooth and focused Hajj experience. The hotel is surrounded by several markets, approximately a 5-minute walk away. Throughout the journey, pilgrims benefit from continuous support and organization provided by a dedicated professional team, culminating in the deeply moving experience of standing on the blessed plains of Arafah, symbolizing repentance, renewal, and a sincere commitment to a new beginning. Accommodation is provided in fully equipped, air-conditioned camps in Arafat and Mina, along with comfortable sleeping bags in Muzdalifah, in addition to airport meet-and-greet services, a complimentary Hajj essentials kit, and the guidance of an experienced and certified tour leader to ensure a seamless and spiritually fulfilling journey. Hotel check-in begins at 5:00 pm and check-out is at 10:00 am. These timings may be adjusted due to operational requirements, and package duration or flight schedules may change accordingly. Triple and quad rooms may include additional rollaway beds and may be relatively compact, while double rooms typically consist of twin beds with no guarantee of a king-size bed. Room-sharing preferences may not always be fulfilled, with strict separation between male and female accommodations. Note: Hotel ratings may vary during the 1447H – 2026 Hajj season. No compensation shall be provided in such cases, as all services will continue to be delivered according to the standards outlined in the package."

Cancelation Policy

  • Full Refund - A 100% of the package amount will be refunded except the custom flights if the cancellation is made within 72 hours from the time of booking and no later than 11:59 PM (Makkah Time) on 1 Dhu al-Qi‘dah 2026-04-27, whichever occurs first.

  • Partial Refund - A 100% of the package amount excluding visa fees and the custom flights will be refunded if the cancellation is made after 72 hours from the time of booking, provided it is no later than 11:59 PM (Makkah Time) on 1 Dhu al-Qi‘dah 2026-04-27.

  • No Refund - No refunds will be issued for cancellations made after 11:59 PM (Makkah Time) on 1 Dhu al-Qi'dah (2026-04-27).

  • Flight Booking Amount - The above rules apply to flight bookings arranged by the service provider and do not apply to custom flight bookings made through the airline system, which are subject to the specific cancellation policies of each booking.

  • Important Notes for Refunds - A processing fee of 2.3% (inclusive of VAT) may be deducted when refunding amounts to digital wallets, in accordance with the refund policy stated in the digital wallet.

  • Fees and exchange rate differences - Currency exchange differences may result in variations between the amounts deposited and refunded to digital wallets.
